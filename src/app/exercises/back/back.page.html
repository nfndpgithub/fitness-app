CHEST EXERCISES

1. Cat Cow

Equipment required: none

  1. Get into a quadruped position (on your hands and knees). Stack your hands directly beneath your shoulders and your knees slightly wider than hips (in line with your hands).

  2. Round your back as much as possible, spreading your shoulder blades and bending your neck down to flex your spine.

  3. Slowly arch your back, squeezing your shoulder blades and looking up to create spinal extension.

  4. Repeat the process between the two extremes, moving deliberately.

2.Band Bent-Over Row

Equipment required: band

  1. Grab a low-resistance band and set it out on the ground.

  2. Stand on the middle of the band, grabbing the two ends in either hand with a pronated (overhand) grip, hinging at the hips and slightly bending your knees in an athletic stance. Make sure that your back isn't rounded.

  3. Squeeze your back to pull the band ends simultaneously to your chest, or as close as the band allows.

  4. Pause for a moment at the top of the motion, then slowly return to the original position, working against the band's resistance.

  5. Do 3 sets of 8–12 repetitions.

3.Pendlay Row

Equipment required: barbell and weights

  1. Step up to a loaded barbell, keeping the barbell close to your shins, standing with feet shoulder-width apart. Tighten your abs, push your butt back, and bend at the waist, lowering until your arms can grasp the bar.

  2. Squeeze your glutes and make sure your hips are lower than your shoulders. Squeeze your shoulder blades.

  3. Maintaining this position, explosively row the barbell to the bottom of your ribcage. Lower back to the ground. Reset your body and repeat.
